In other words

Data mining is when we use technology to search through a lot of data and find important patterns and information. It's like sifting through a big pile of information to find the most valuable pieces. To do this, we first get the data ready by organizing and transforming it. Then, we use different tools and methods, like drawing pictures or using smart computer programs, to help us uncover hidden connections and learn new things from the data. Data mining is important because it allows businesses to make better decisions based on what they discover from the data. It helps them find important relationships and gain an edge over their competitors. In the world of data science, data mining is a powerful tool that helps us explore and make sense of large amounts of data, so we can find valuable knowledge that can make a difference.
